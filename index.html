About The Conference

AAWIL in 2020

2020 has brought unprecedented challenges in the world and a new willingness to address the need for change. Recognizing that we come from different places and experiences, this is a time to unite in solidarity and offer hope. How will we look to our past and to our present to reshape what is ahead? As we move forward, how will we Reimagine Our Future? Through the lens of Asian American women, the 2020 AAWIL Conference aims to envision and assert our voice and identity as a community in this interconnected, diverse world. How will we reclaim our own definition of who we are, breaking free from societal expectations, stereotypes, and barriers set upon people of color? As individuals and as a collective, how will we Become The Change that we want to see?

Rotating Speed Networking / Mini Mentoring

Meet new people, develop connections, and gain insight regarding the next phase of their educational or professional career. Each networking group will have an industry lead to help mentor and provide guidance. There will be two breakout groups of 20 minutes each and on day two groups get to dive even deeper to discuss how imposter syndrome could be affecting their career as an Asian American. We hope to provide real advice on career problems that are often overlooked from someone leading in their industry. Lets have meaningful conversations together!

This workshop aims to provide a safe, comfortable environment where participants can share their experiences with mental health and learn about ways in which they can cope and empower themselves.
