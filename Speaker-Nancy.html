Panel: Diversity In Careers

Nancy A. Lee

Nancy A. Lee is the founder and managing attorney of Lee & Associates, P.C. based in Quincy, Massachusetts. Nancy concentrates her practice in commercial and residential real estate matters (including acquisitions, sales, financing, and leasing) and small to mid-sized business formation. In 2015, Nancy was named a "" by New England Super Lawyers. Nancy was also just recently honored as one of the 2019 Top Women of Law by Massachusetts Lawyers Weekly.

Nancy is a graduate of Smith College, an all women's college, and Northeastern University School of Law. While in law school, Nancy served as a judicial intern to the Honorable Marianne B. Bowler, United States District Court of Massachusetts, and was a legal intern at Nixon Peabody LLP, Davis, Malm & D'Agostine P.C., and the Federal Reserve Bank of Boston. Prior to starting Lee & Associates, P.C., Nancy was an associate attorney at law firms in Boston and Newton, Massachusetts.

In her spare time, Nancy is a guest speaker at home buying seminars for the Asian Community Development Corporation (ACDC), Quincy Community Action Programs, Inc. (QCAP), the Allston Brighton Community Development Corporation and other first time home buying seminars in the Greater Boston area. Nancy currently serves on the Board of Directors for the Boston chapter of the National Association of Asian American Professionals (NAAAP) and as an Advisory Board Member for the Boston chapter of the Asian Real Estate Association of America (AREAA).

The AAWIL Conference, organized by ASPIRE, is an interactive, one-day event aimed at growing and developing leadership skills. The program includes a keynote address, shared discussions, small group activities, networking opportunities, workshops, and training sessions.
